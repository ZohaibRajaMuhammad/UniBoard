# Design System Foundation

## Visual Identity
- The product uses a dark, polished academic workspace aesthetic.
- The interface should feel more like a premium intelligence console than a casual student forum.
- AI is a first-class design citizen and should feel embedded, not bolted on.

## Color System
- Base background: near-black midnight slate
- Elevated backgrounds: layered dark navy/slate surfaces
- Primary accent: sapphire blue
- Secondary prestige accent: champagne gold
- Support accents: green for success, red for urgency, violet for AI or analytical emphasis, sky for informational data

## Typography
- Primary font: Plus Jakarta Sans
- Mono font: JetBrains Mono
- Headline style: light-to-medium weight with tight tracking on premium surfaces
- Body style: medium legibility, low-fatigue line height
- Metadata style: smaller, dimmer, more compressed

## Surface Language
- Primary surface type: glass-like dark cards
- Border treatment: thin, low-contrast, luminous when active
- Shadows: soft depth, never heavy blur like consumer dashboards
- Radius language: medium to large radii for calm professionalism

## Density Rules
- The UI is information-dense but not cramped.
- Every screen must retain whitespace channels between content groups.
- Dense lists should still maintain hierarchy through chip color, weight, and spacing.

## Design Principles
1. Academic clarity over decorative excess
2. Premium dark surfaces over flat monochrome blocks
3. AI visibility without UI noise
4. Strong hierarchy between urgent, analytical, and conversational content
5. Responsive compression without losing scan order
