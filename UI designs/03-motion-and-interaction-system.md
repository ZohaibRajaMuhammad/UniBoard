# Motion And Interaction System

## Motion Personality
- Motion should feel premium and responsive, not playful
- Hover should suggest intelligence and precision
- Overlay motion should feel soft and controlled
- AI surfaces may feel slightly more alive than standard utility panels

## Timing
- Micro interactions: 150ms to 180ms
- Standard transitions: 200ms to 240ms
- Page or panel entrances: 280ms to 320ms
- Spring overlays: restrained, not bouncy

## Interaction Types
- Hover lift on cards
- Border-brightening on active surfaces
- Soft glow for focused inputs
- Subtle transform on primary CTA hover
- Compact pulse for AI entry points

## Motion Constraints
- Avoid long parallax
- Avoid attention-stealing motion loops except small AI pulse
- Avoid excessive stagger that slows work
- Support reduced motion mode cleanly

## Required States
- default
- hover
- focus
- active
- loading
- success
- error
- disabled
- empty
- stale or degraded when data is old
