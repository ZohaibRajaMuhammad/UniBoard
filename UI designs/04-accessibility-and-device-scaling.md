# Accessibility And Device Scaling

## Accessibility Rules
- All icon-only buttons must have labels
- All focusable controls must show visible focus
- Color alone must never carry critical meaning
- Motion must have a reduced-motion-safe version
- Text contrast must meet WCAG AA at minimum

## Touch Rules
- Minimum touch target: 44x44px
- Minimum spacing between mobile tap targets: 8px
- Inputs must remain easy to activate under thumb interaction

## Content Rules
- Long text must wrap gracefully
- Feed cards must support zoom without overflow breakage
- Analytical cards must remain understandable at 200% zoom

## Device Scaling Requirements
- Large desktop must not look sparse
- Small laptop must not look cramped
- Tablet must preserve task priority
- Mobile must preserve action priority

## AI Accessibility
- AI-generated output blocks must remain readable
- AI labels should be visually distinct but not over-bright
- AI errors and low-confidence states must be explicit in text
